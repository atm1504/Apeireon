About Us

Apeireon, the codefest of IIT PATNA

Apeireon is the annual Technical fest of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering of IIT Patna. It is not just an old school departmental fest but will be packed with a multitude of fun events and workshops to help you develop technical skills, brainstorm and learn in an exciting way. It is an effort to promote technical and to give students, an immersive exposure and opportunity towards the various aspects of Computer Science.

Flagship CP Contest

(5-6 PROBLEMS) Regular CP Contest

Shortest Code

(5-6 PROBLEMS) In this event, the contestants shall be required to solve each challenge but their score would depend the number of characters in their code (the shorter the code, higher their score).

Mystery Language

( 5-6 problems ) This event would be quite similar to a regular competitive contest but with a twist, the participants shall be required to code in a surprise language that would be revealed only moments before the contest begins. This way they may have to learn an entire language while giving the contest.

Hacking Challenge

(6-8 problems) In this event the participants shall be provided with a problem statement as well as a partially correct algorithm. They would be required to find any possible input for which the algorithm provided to them fails.

Art of Counterfeiting

A video of a website/app will be shown to the participants. Participants will have to replicate the website/app (both UI and function) in a limited amount of time. Images and other media used in the video will be provided. Possibly from a design document

ML Contest

Participants shall be given a dataset to make a prediction based learning model using some basic classification or clustering algorithms. Solutions will be ranked according to their accuracy

"Linux Desktop Customization"

Participants have to submit a screenshot (max 4) of their desktop along with the config files. They can also give a short description. The images will be posted on social media and voting will be done. The best entry will be chosen based on the votes.

"DynamicDash"

Participants would participate in teams of upto 3 members and they will have to start from the initial point. To reach the final point, they have to solve problems related to programming in order to proceed ahead.

CodeSprint-360

60 Very easy questions will be given to solve in 3 hours.Race to Win

IoT Design

A presentation of a theoritical design of an IoT device, maybe drone, maybe a smart device, maybe something entirely different

CC mei ICPC

This event is similar to real ACM ICPC. Code to get ballons
